Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta

This is a simple creamy pasta made with chicken and garlic. It is tasty, easy to cook, and perfect for beginners. Many people enjoy this dish because it is very comforting.

Recipe Image

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (chopped)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta and drain it.
  2. Melt butter in a pan.
  3. Add garlic and cook for a few seconds.
  4. Add the chicken and stir it.
  5. Pour in heavy cream and let it cook.
  6. Mix the pasta with the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.

Tips

You can add cheese for extra flavour. Adding parsley makes it look nicer. For stronger garlic taste, add a little extra garlic.
